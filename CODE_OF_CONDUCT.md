# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participate respectfully, assume good intent, and keep discussion focused on the work.

Examples of expected behavior:

- clear technical disagreement without personal attacks
- respectful review feedback
- honest attribution of risks, blockers, and unknowns

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory language
- bad-faith disruption of collaboration spaces

## Enforcement

Report issues privately to the maintainers through the contact path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) or the repository owners listed on GitHub.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict access when behavior crosses the line.
